Frequently Asked Questions

What is TrueBot?

TrueBot is an AI-powered chatbot built with the Google Gemini API. It can understand context, answer questions, and hold a natural conversation.

Is my conversation private?

Yes, your conversation is temporary and is not stored permanently. The chat history is only used to maintain context during the current session.

Can TrueBot remember previous conversations?

Currently, TrueBot only remembers the context of the ongoing conversation. It will start fresh if you reload the page.

How do I format my messages?

Just type your message naturally. If the AI's response should be a list, it will format it point-by-point for you automatically.
